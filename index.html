As the containment of 3 billion people is nearing completion, a mobile application can limit the emergence of a second wave. By recording the proximity of its users, it can calculate a risk score.

Some suggest that this calculation should be done by companies or authorities. Instead, we propose to perform your own calculation, for yourself, in your smartphone. This way, your personal data will never escape your control.

Ask questions! Get involved

OpenStopCovid is designed to respect your privacy.
